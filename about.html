History

At Savvy Coders, we began with a vision of reaching out to people traditionally underrepresented in the technology field. Partnering with several non-profit groups, we endeavored to give back to our communities by increasing tech talent and expanding business opportunities. We designed a comprehensive curriculum that would allow our students to expand their professional horizons in 12 weeks.

With the values of quality, flexibility, and accessibility in mind, we offer courses open to anyone with a desire to learn. We challenge ourselves to meet the following goals:

  • To make the tech field accessible to everyone, especially those underrepresented in the industry.
  • To help communities succeed by providing tech talent that growing businesses need.
  • To give students a comprehensive, fundamental understanding of software development skills including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Firebase, Agile and more.
  • To provide more value in this short time frame than other programs do in months, and help our students with internships, networking, and job placement post-graduation.
